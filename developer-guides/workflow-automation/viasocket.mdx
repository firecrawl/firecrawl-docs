---
title: "Firecrawl + viaSocket"
description: "Connect Firecrawl with viaSocket for seamless workflow automation and task management"
---

Follow these steps to connect your Firecrawl and automate your tasks effortlessly.

**Step 1:** Login to viaSocket

**Step 2:** Inside the viaSocket panel > Connections > Choose "Connect new apps"

**Step 3 :** Use the search bar to locate Firecrawl from the list of available apps.

![Your](https://storage.googleapis.com/techdoc.walkover.in/23nmZIclc6pr/vsI3MPBel9wB/9Xk1t46YIrEj/40253438-ab59-498a-b9d3-c11b41fad8d2_Your%20paragraph%20text%20(36)_compressed.png)

**Step 4:** Established the Connection : By entering the API Key.

Login to Firecrawl > go to the API Keys and copy the key.

![Your](https://storage.googleapis.com/techdoc.walkover.in/23nmZIclc6pr/vsI3MPBel9wB/9Xk1t46YIrEj/cae9585e-2634-4280-8ede-a7f8cde1cb68_Your%20paragraph%20text%20(38)_compressed.png)

**Step 5:** After entering the API key , your connection is successfully established.

![Your](https://storage.googleapis.com/techdoc.walkover.in/23nmZIclc6pr/vsI3MPBel9wB/9Xk1t46YIrEj/84e4741b-c901-4be6-b999-4f422f481b25_Your%20paragraph%20text%20(40)_compressed.png)

You've successfully created the connection and can now use the Firecrawl app in your flows.

If you want to make changes to your connection in the future, follow the steps here.

> You can edit your connections after you create them. Click Edit to open a sidebar where you can fill in the new data for the connection.
