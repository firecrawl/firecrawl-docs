Describe what you need in a prompt and `/agent` searches, navigates, and extracts data from across the web. No URLs required, no scripts, no manual work.

<Info>
**Research Preview**: Agent is in early access. Expect rough edges. It will get significantly better over time. [Share feedback →](mailto:product@firecrawl.com)
</Info>

- **No URLs required**: Just describe what you need via the `prompt` parameter. URLs are optional.
- **Deep web search**: Autonomously searches and navigates deep into sites to find your data.
- **Reliable and accurate**: Works with a wide variety of queries and use cases.
- **Parallel processing**: Processes multiple sources in parallel for faster results.

## Async job status

Agent jobs run asynchronously. When you submit a job, you receive a job ID that you can use to check status.

- **Default method**: `agent()` waits and returns final results.
- **Start then poll**: Use `start_agent` (Python) or `startAgent` (Node) to get a job ID immediately, then poll with `get_agent_status` / `getAgentStatus`.

<Note>Job results are available via the API for 24 hours after completion. After this period, you can still view your agent history and results in the [activity logs](https://www.firecrawl.dev/app/logs).</Note>

## Model selection

Firecrawl Agent offers two models. **Spark 1 Mini is 60% cheaper** and is the default, suitable for most use cases. Upgrade to Spark 1 Pro when you need maximum accuracy on complex tasks.

| Model | Cost | Accuracy | Best for |
|-------|------|----------|----------|
| `spark-1-mini` | **60% cheaper** | Standard | Most tasks (default) |
| `spark-1-pro` | Standard | Higher | Complex research, critical extraction |

<Tip>
**Start with Spark 1 Mini** (default). It handles most extraction tasks well at 60% lower cost. Switch to Pro only for complex multi-domain research or when accuracy is critical.
</Tip>

### Spark 1 Mini (default)

`spark-1-mini` is the efficient model, ideal for straightforward data extraction tasks.

**Use Mini when:**
- Extracting simple data points (contact info, pricing, etc.)
- Working with well-structured websites
- Cost efficiency is a priority
- Running high-volume extraction jobs

### Spark 1 Pro

`spark-1-pro` is the flagship model, designed for maximum accuracy on complex extraction tasks.

**Use Pro when:**
- Performing complex competitive analysis
- Extracting data that requires deep reasoning
- Accuracy is critical for your use case
- Dealing with ambiguous or hard-to-find data

## Parameters

| Parameter | Type | Required | Description |
|-----------|------|----------|-------------|
| `prompt` | `string` | **Yes** | Natural language description of the data you want to extract (max 10,000 characters) |
| `model` | `string` | No | Model to use: `spark-1-mini` (default) or `spark-1-pro` |
| `urls` | `array` | No | List of URLs to focus the extraction on |
| `schema` | `object` | No | JSON schema for structured output |
| `maxCredits` | `number` | No | Maximum credits to spend on this agent task. Defaults to **2,500**. If the limit is reached, the job fails and no data is returned, though credits consumed for work already performed are still charged. |

## CSV upload in Agent Playground

The [Agent Playground](https://www.firecrawl.dev/app/agent) supports CSV upload for batch processing. Upload a CSV containing your input data (company names, URLs, or any entities), write a prompt describing what data you want the agent to find for each row, define your output fields, and run. The agent processes each row in parallel and fills in the results.

## Pricing

Agent uses **dynamic billing** that scales with the complexity of your request. You pay based on the actual work performed.

| Scenario | Typical cost |
|----------|-------------|
| Simple extractions (contact info from a single page) | Fewer credits |
| Complex research (competitive analysis across multiple domains) | More credits |
| Parallel agents with Spark-1 Fast | 10 credits per cell |

<Info>
Most agent runs consume **a few hundred credits**. Simpler single-page tasks may use less and complex multi-domain research may use more.
</Info>

### Free tier

All users receive **5 free daily runs**, usable from either the playground or the API. Additional usage is billed based on credit consumption.

### Managing costs

- **Set `maxCredits`**: Limit spending by setting a maximum credit budget per task.
- **Optimize prompts**: More specific prompts often use fewer credits.
- **Monitor usage**: Track consumption through the [dashboard](https://www.firecrawl.dev/app).
- **Start small**: Use your 5 daily free runs to understand pricing for your use cases.

Try Agent now at [firecrawl.dev/app/agent](https://www.firecrawl.dev/app/agent) to see how credit usage scales with your specific use cases.

<Note>
Pricing is subject to change as Agent moves from Research Preview to general availability. Current users will receive advance notice of any pricing updates.
</Note>
