---
title: "Delete a webhook"
---

Webhooks are tied to the lifecycle of a job — there is no separate delete endpoint.

A webhook is automatically removed when its parent job reaches a terminal state (`completed`, `failed`, or `cancelled`). You can also stop receiving webhooks early by cancelling the job:

- **Crawl:** [`DELETE /crawl/{id}`](/api-reference/endpoint/crawl-cancel)
- **Agent:** [`DELETE /agent/{id}`](/api-reference/endpoint/agent-delete)

Batch scrape jobs cannot be cancelled; the webhook remains active until the job completes.

All pending webhook deliveries for a cancelled job are discarded.
