## Overview

Lovable apps can use Firecrawl to search the web and scrape pages into clean, LLM-ready content while you build.

## Quick start

1. Open your Lovable project
2. Follow Lovable's Firecrawl integration guide to connect your workspace
3. Ask Lovable to search or scrape with Firecrawl for the data your app needs

## What you can build

| Capability | Use case |
|------------|----------|
| **Search** | Find live sources for research and enrichment |
| **Scrape** | Pull clean markdown or structured data from any URL |
| **Extract** | Turn pages into typed fields for your app UI |
